# Employee Attrition Prediction - Classification

# Dataset Information

This dataset contains information about employees working in a company, including demographic, job-related, and satisfaction factors. They aim to predict whether an employee will leave the company (Attrition = Yes) or stay (Attrition = No), helping organizations take data-driven actions to reduce attrition and improve employee retention.

This is a standard supervised classification task. A classification problem where we have to predict whether an employee will leave the organization or not. Below is the dataset attributes with description.

Variable	Description
Age	Age of the employee
Gender	Male/ Female
Department	Employee’s department (HR, Sales, R&D, etc.)
JobRole	Role/designation of the employee
MonthlyIncome	Monthly salary of the employee
JobSatisfaction	Satisfaction level (1–4)
WorkLifeBalance	Rating of work-life balance (1–4)
OverTime	Whether employee works overtime (Yes/No)
YearsAtCompany	Total years spent in the company
DistanceFromHome	Distance between home and office
Attrition	Target variable – whether the employee left the company (Yes/No)

Download link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/pavansubhasht/ibm-hr-analytics-attrition-dataset
